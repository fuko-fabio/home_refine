Multilingual Accounting

W-IT Services Limited is a professional multilingual accounting firm registered under the Professional Accountants’  Association of Chartered Certified Accountant (“ACCA”) in the UK. It provides a wide range of multilingual accounting services. Founded in 2003; it focus on multilingual accountant services, providing an integrated range to the UK or European based Companies who have foreign subsidiaries in Japan, China and Hong Kong or have an intention to expand the new market in these territories.

Our directors have extensive experiences in working from SMEs and Multi-national Corporations in terms of incorporation, taxation, business advisory, system integration consultancy, merger and acquisition, transfer pricing, company secretary, and corporate and group restructuring exercises.
